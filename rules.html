Rules

The In Season Trophy is a season long game in the NHL. 1 Team starts with the trophy and holds on to it until another team beats them. After each win, a single point is given to the team that is holding the trophy. The team that starts with the trophy at the beginning of the 2021-2022 season is the Tampa Bay Lightning, because they won the 2021 Stanley Cup. At the end of the season there will be 2 winners, the team with the most points, and the team that finishes the season with the cup.
